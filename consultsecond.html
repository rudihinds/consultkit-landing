FAQ

What if I can’t deliver what the report recommends?
The report distinguishes between existing tools you can resell and configure, and custom builds that need development work. You see which is which, with pricing guidance for both. You decide what to deliver yourself and what to scope as a build.
How do I actually get prospects to take the audit?
The same places you’re already trying to find clients — just with something that actually converts. Drop the link in LinkedIn DMs, outreach emails, your website, ads, or conversations at events. It works everywhere because prospects aren’t being asked to “book a call with a stranger.” They’re being offered a free AI audit that shows them where they’re leaving money on the table. That’s a fundamentally different ask — and it converts whether traffic is cold, warm, or paid.
How is this different from just using ChatGPT?
ChatGPT gives generic advice from a single prompt. ConsultKit runs a structured audit, then researches what’s actually being deployed in your prospect’s industry right now — real tools, real pricing, real ROI. The output is a professional report your client can act on, white-labeled under your brand, with your pipeline filling in the background. Different category of tool entirely.
Do I need to be an AI expert to use this?
No. The system handles the research and analysis. By the time you see the output, it’s a business-language report telling you what to recommend and what to charge. If you can walk a client through “here’s what’s costing you money, here’s what would fix it, here’s what it costs” — you’re qualified.
Can I see what the report looks like before I commit?
Yes. The 14-day free trial gives you full access — generate audits, see reports, explore the dashboard and recommended packages. Test it with a real prospect or run it yourself before you pay anything.
What if other consultants in my space use ConsultKit too?
Every report is generated fresh from live research — not templates. Recommendations follow the business, not the consultant. But that’s the point: the value isn’t in the tool, it’s in the relationship and the ability to close. ConsultKit gives everyone the same quality of diagnosis. What you do with it is what differentiates you.
Is the white-label actually convincing?
Fully. Your logo, your colours, your landing page. The audit, the report, and the booking flow all run under your brand. No “Powered by” watermark. From your client’s perspective, you built this.
Will prospects actually complete a 14-question audit?
They will — because it answers a question they already have: “Where is my business leaving money on the table?” The report they get back names specific tools with projected ROI, not generic advice. That quality of output is why people finish it. Over 750 business leaders across 38 countries have completed it.
What happens after 10 audits?
Extra audits are $12 each — no limit. If you’re running more than 10 per month, your pipeline is full and each audit costs less than a coffee. We’ll introduce higher-volume plans as demand grows.
Can I pause or cancel anytime?
Yes. Pause for up to 3 months — your data, brand settings, and lead history are preserved. Or cancel anytime. No contracts, no commitment, no questions.
